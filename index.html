About VeloGrit Games

Velogrit Games is a passionate indie development studio of young and talented individuals. Based in Lexington, Kentucky, we reach out to developers local to the area and use our team to learn and grow to give you the best product we can. We strive to give other peers around us opportunities to experience the industry, and publish something of their own.
